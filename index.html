Frequently asked

VenueSumo reviews, summarised

The questions operators actually ask before they book a demo — answered from real reviewer feedback.

Is VenueSumo good for trampoline parks and family entertainment centres?
Yes. VenueSumo is purpose-built for attractions and leisure venues including amusement parks, aquariums, arcades, axe throwing, bowling alleys, escape rooms, family entertainment centres, go-karts, indoor play centres, laser tag, mini-golf, museums, pickleball, rock climbing centres, tourism attractions, trampoline parks, virtual reality centres, water parks and zoos. Verified Capterra reviewers from bike parks, zoos, FECs and arena venues consistently rate it 4 or 5 stars.
What is VenueSumo's average review rating?
VenueSumo is rated 4.9 out of 5 stars on Capterra, based on 11 verified operator reviews, plus 5.0 / 5 on Google. Sub-scores: 4.8 for ease of use and 4.9 for customer service. Last updated March 13, 2026 — see the live Capterra profile.
Does VenueSumo integrate with OTAs and channel managers?
Yes. VenueSumo connects to leading travel and booking platforms including Expedia, Viator and Tripadvisor, plus walk-ups and your own website — all from one calendar. Availability, pricing and bookings stay automatically synced. See the channel management feature.
How long does VenueSumo take to set up?
Typically 4–6 weeks to ensure your data is migrated accurately and payments are connected. The VenueSumo team builds your setup, migrates your data, and provides 1:1 training so your team is confident from day one. Gary D. (Capterra, May 2024) called out the integration process with merchant solutions as "very good."
Can VenueSumo replace my POS, booking and waiver systems?
Yes — that's the design intent. VenueSumo unifies online bookings, POS, parties, memberships, waivers, payments, CRM and OTAs into one cloud-based platform. Chris B. (Capterra) describes it as the platform that lets him "tailor business management software and customise it to suit our needs — point of sale, reporting, event management, ticketing and customer management."
Does VenueSumo work for multi-venue and franchise operators?
Yes. Multi-site customers including BounceINC and Flip Out Australia use VenueSumo as their group-wide platform. As BounceINC's Bree put it: "The minute we got our last venue across to VenueSumo, we breathed a sigh of relief. Everything we sell, in-store and online, now runs through one system."
How does VenueSumo compare to ROLLER?
Five of the eleven verified Capterra reviewers explicitly switched to VenueSumo from ROLLER, citing local support, transparent pricing, and ease of use. One reviewer wrote: "Roller is the worst company I've ever dealt with in my 30 year career. Every time I ask about a feature, it's not included, everything is an upsell."
